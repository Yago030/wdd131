Website Plan Triadesoft

Site Name

Triadesoft is a software development company driven by purpose and built on solid roots. While we work with all kinds of businesses, we have a deep commitment to supporting rural entrepreneurs, cooperatives, and local NGOs. We believe in using technology to create meaningful impact — empowering communities through scalable, thoughtful solutions.

Site Purpose

The goal of this website is to professionally present Triadesoft, highlight the services we offer, and connect with projects that need digital solutions with real-world impact. The site will serve as a landing page to reach potential clients, showcase case studies, and invite collaboration — especially from cooperatives, NGOs, and rural ventures.

Use Scenarios

  • Agricultural entrepreneur: "I produce artisanal honey and want to sell online, but I don’t know where to start. Can Triadesoft help me set up my online store?"
  • Rural educational NGO: "We've been working with children in the countryside for 10 years, but no one knows our impact. We need to show what we do to gain more donations."
  • Dairy cooperative: "We are 30 producers working together and still manage everything on paper. We want a system to help us organize and sell directly to consumers."
  • Agroecological project: "We grow organic and want to educate about sustainable agriculture. We need a website to tell our story and sell our products."

Color Palette

  • --color-primary: #1A2332 — Deep blue, trust and professionalism
  • --color-accent: #00B4A6 — Tech green, growth and innovation
  • --color-success: #22C55E — Nature green, positive impact
  • --color-earth: #8B7355 — Earth brown, our rural roots
  • --color-bg: #FFFFFF — Clean white, clarity and simplicity

Five colors that meet accessibility standards and reflect our identity: technology rooted in the land.

Typography

  • --font-headings: 'Poppins' — Clear and bold headings
  • --font-primary: 'Inter' — Readable body text on any device

Wireframes

Simple, functional design with a mobile-first approach:

  • Mobile: Logo, clean menu, clear sections, large buttons
  • Desktop: Header with navigation, multi-column layout, spacious content
